See the stars at Bunnar Springs Observatory

Bunnar Springs is one of the darkest places in the country. We rank as the 2nd lowest location in Australia on the LPI (light pollution index). At night, the stars are crystal clear when viewed from the Observatory.

We are open to the public thanks to support from Clarcona University.
